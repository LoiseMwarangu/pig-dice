Objectives

You will roll a six sided die.The main objective of this game is to be the first player to reach 100 points.Try hard not to roll a one since this means that you loose your current score


General Rules

In each turn a player rolls a die repeatedly until either a 1 is rolled or player decides to hold and pass on the turn to its opponent.

The number player rolls is added to a interim score. If the player rolls a 1, they lose their interim score and turn is passed to their opponent.

If player gets any other number, they can decide to continue and roll the die.

If player decides to hold, then their turn total (interim score) is added to the overall score.

The first player to score 100 points wins the game.
